Approach To Care

Kidney care that balances clinical precision with long-term continuity.

Dr. Vathsalyan's care model is built around careful evaluation, stage-wise management, and ongoing review rather than one-time treatment decisions. The focus is not only on treating disease, but also on slowing progression, reducing complications, and helping patients live better with chronic conditions.

  • Structured follow-up for chronic kidney disease and other renal disorders.
  • Practical outpatient guidance for dialysis, transplant follow-up, and recurrent kidney issues.
  • Comfort-focused planning for advanced kidney disease when the clinical situation demands it.

Onco-nephrology is a defining overlap in his work.

Patients undergoing cancer treatment may develop kidney injury, electrolyte problems, or treatment-related renal complications. Dr. Vathsalyan's role inside a dual-speciality clinic makes these issues easier to evaluate and follow over time.

Patient-first review Clear follow-up plans and continuity matter as much as diagnosis.
