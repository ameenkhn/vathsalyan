Approach To Care

Holistic cancer care with plain-language guidance and support for families.

Dr. Sivayogini's style of care places communication at the center of oncology. The treatment journey includes more than therapy selection. It also includes counselling, symptom support, realistic explanation of options, and follow-up planning that helps patients understand what comes next.

  • Thoughtful evaluation of diagnosis, reports, imaging, and staging before treatment planning.
  • Systemic therapy oversight alongside supportive care and quality of life considerations.
  • Counselling and shared decision-making for patients and families throughout treatment.
