FAQ

What services does Aadhiran Speciality Clinic offer?
The clinic offers kidney care led by Dr. P. Vathsalyan and cancer care led by Dr. S. Sivayogini, including onco-nephrology support where both areas overlap.
What are the clinic's working hours?
Consultations are available Monday to Saturday from 5:30 PM to 8:30 PM.
Do you treat chronic kidney disease and acute kidney injury?
Yes. The nephrology pathway includes CKD management, acute kidney injury, nephrotic and nephritic syndromes, kidney stones, urinary infections, and transplant follow-up.
Does the clinic provide dialysis-related outpatient support?
Yes. Hemodialysis OPD services and Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD) OPD services are part of the nephrology offering.
What oncology services are available?
Cancer evaluation and diagnosis, systemic therapy, multidisciplinary coordination, supportive care, survivorship care, palliative oncology, counselling, vaccinations, and genetic counselling are all part of the oncology pathway.
Can the clinic manage kidney complications during cancer treatment?
Yes. Onco-nephrology is an important shared service for kidney injury, electrolyte problems, and other renal complications related to cancer therapy.
Do you provide palliative care?
Yes. Both palliative nephrology and palliative oncology are part of the clinic's care model, with emphasis on comfort, symptom relief, emotional support, and quality of life.
Where is the clinic located?
Aadhiran Speciality Clinic is located at No. 2, Sarvodhaya 3rd Street, Opposite Damro Showroom, 70 Feet Road, New Ellis Nagar, Madurai - 625016.
